The Treasury Act  
To ensure a stable economy, government funding, and a strong currency.

**Article I \- The Fim**  
Section 1: The Fim is the official currency of Fuhai, and Fuhai alone has sole control over the management of the Fim.

Section 2:  Upon the passing of this act, the Treasury Manager (Treasurer), as defined by the People and Places Act, will mint 6,000 additional Fim to achieve 1,000Fim/capita to match the per capita rate Fuhai’s Founders initially minted with the new population size.

**Article II \- Government Funding**  
Section 1: To avoid direct taxation, and to ensure a stable healthy inflation rate for the Fim, the governmental budget will come from yearly currency minting. The Mint% is started at 12% and may be changed by the President or Treasurer, to reflect current national need. The yearly mint is to be performed by the Treasurer within a week of the Presidential Election, the amount to be minted is the Mint% of the total Fim not held by the Treasury.  

Section 2: The Treasurer is responsible for approving government expenses and budgeting, if  
denied by the Treasurer, an expense or budget may be escalated to the president.

Section 3: The Justice Enforcer, Transportation and Communication Manager, and Technology and Infrastructure Manager, as defined by the People and Places act, are each guaranteed 9% of the amount minted to fund their government department, this budget is internally managed by the department. The Treasurer is responsible for providing additional funding to these departments as needed to best serve Fuhai. The Treasurer may also provide funding for private entities to strengthen the economy and improve Fuhai. 

Section 4: The Mint% is to be public knowledge.

Section 5: The Treasurer is responsible for paying all government officials, as defined by the People and Places Act, a salary of 70 Fim a year. 

**Article III \- Banking**  
Section 1: Banks operating in Fuhai must register with the Treasury Department. To register the bank must have a physical location, existing banks may register by arranging with the Treasurer to secure a physical location within an agreed timeframe. 

Section 2: Banks must comply with Fuhai court ordered seizures, and audits.

Section 3: Government payouts to non foreign entities must be made to Fuhai Registered Banks.

**Article IV \- The Treasury Department**   
Section 1: The Treasury Department is headed by the Treasury Manager as defined by the People and Places act.

Section 2:  The Treasury Department is defined as a stand alone department of the government responsible for managing resources and currency, maintaining the economy and auditing government spending.

Section 3: To begin balancing government power The Treasury Department’s powers and responsibility may not be handled by The President of Fuhai (TPOF), or any other government official besides the Treasury Manager. If TPOF believes the Treasury Manager is no longer able to perform their duties, they may remove them with unanimous approval of all other government officials defined in the People and Places act.
