The New Nation Act


Article I
Section 1: The Fuhai Criminal Code will define crimes, their classifications and punishments.


Section 2: The Fuhai Criminal Code is a dynamic document that may be updated the same as passing a new law. The public is to be notified the same as if a new law is passed.




Article II
Section 1: Those seeking to immigrate to Fuhai with a criminal history, whether a crime of the nation they were in, or an act that would have been considered a crime in Fuhai, are judged on a case by case basis for citizenship.


Section 2: Citizenship may be denied to those believed to be an enemy of the state, or with direct ties to an enemy of the state, or those who may otherwise be a national security risk.


Section 3: Citizenship may be delayed in the early days of the nation while private ownership is determined, and government property is separated.

Section 4: Out of Character vibe check, players wanting to join should be willing to roleplay, be respectful and mature, value the whole story and lore not just their own, not power game, and bring good vibes to a largely queer neurodivergent community.


Article III
Section 1: Builds that will use Copycats™ must receive a permit, detailing the amount and their usage. 

Section 2: Builds left unsightly or unfinished for 1 year* may be subject to demolition, attempts to contact the owner to arrange a timeline for finishing must first be made. 


Article IV
Section 1: Land ownership is confirmed by deeds, which are issued by the government, stating; owner, coordinates of opposite corners, and address. 

Section 2:  Land Ownership by default is from Y:20 to Y:200, to own above or below this you must acquire separate air rights or mineral rights from the government.
